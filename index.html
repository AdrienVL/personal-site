About Myself

I'm a French-American based out of San Antonio, Texas. Born and raised in Houston, I graduated from the Awty International School of Houston with the IB diploma. Afterwards, I attended Trinity University in San Antonio where I graduated with a degree in Earth System Science with honors. During my time at Trinity, I competed as a male diver, partook in many volunteering events as a resident of a community service hall, and discovered my passion for programming, inevitably leading me to attend Codeup, a full-stack web development career accelerator.

Professional Perspective

A logic driven software developer, my experience mapping drilling prospects in the Permian Basin combined with my desire to verify product quality, speaks to my approach to solving problems: to plan out, solve, and vigorously test end results. I look forward to being part of a team that honors face to face communication and promotes inclusive growth. My problem-solving mindset, in conjunction with my strong work ethic to find solutions to problems, pushes me to contribute to this disruptive and fast changing technology community.
